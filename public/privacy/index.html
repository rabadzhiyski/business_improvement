Privacy Policy

Introduction

DATA SCIENCE AI LTD, a limited liability corporation incorporated in Bulgaria (the EU), having its registered office located at Sofia, Bulgaria, provides consulting services (“Services”)  to assist organisations. DATA SCIENCE AI LTD has set out the following Policy in relation to data protection.

Purpose

This Policy explains how DATA SCIENCE AI LTD collects, handles, shares, or protects personal data about you under the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) n°2016/679. This Policy also provides information about your rights and how you can contact us if you have questions about the processing of your personal information.

Scope

This Policy applies to individuals who access, use, and/or interact with our products, services, websites, digital properties; to individuals who attend our events online or on-site; and to individual partners that we contract with. Together, these individuals are referred to as “You”. We do not collect personal information about individuals under the age of thirteen (13). This Policy does not apply when DATA SCIENCE AI LTD is acting in the role or capacity of a processor (or any other similar role or capacity) of personal data on behalf of a third-party (client). For instance, the purpose for which our Services are used is defined by our clients and may include answering regulatory obligations or managing risks. In such case, we are processing personal information at the direction and instruction of our client and our client’s privacy policy will govern and control how personal data is processed and protected.

Collection, Purpose, Legal Basis, and Retention Period.

DATA SCIENCE AI LTD collects information through the course of marketing the Services, contracting with clients and partners, or administrating the business. With regards to marketing the Services, DATA SCIENCE AI LTD collects the following personal data: first and last name, company represented, job position, professional email, professional telephone number, professional address.

The personal data is collected by us through your interaction with the website or contacting us via email. The purpose of the personal data collection is to send newsletters, respond to queries, improve website content, enhance marketing strategy, and conducting research and analysis.

The legal basis for collection is the legitimate interest for business development purposes and Services’ improvement. The personal data is retained for a necessary period of time to fulfil the purposes we collected it for. With regards to contracting with clients and partners, DATA SCIENCE AI LTD collects the following personal data: first and last name, company represented, job position, professional email, professional telephone number, professional address, correspondences.

The personal data is collected in the course of contracting or performing the contract. The purpose of the personal data collection is to register new clients/partners, accept orders, fulfil the Services, provide client/partner support.

The legal basis for collection is the contract to perform the Services. The personal data is retained for a necessary period of time to fulfil the purposes we collected it for and comply with applicable laws.

Lastly, with regard to administrating the business, DATA SCIENCE AI LTD collects the following personal data: first and last name, company represented, job position, professional email, professional telephone number, professional address. The personal data is collected during the course of the business relationship.

The purpose of the personal data collection is to comply with legal and financial compliance, as well as invoice and manage payments. The legal basis for collection is the legal obligation for legal, financial, and tax purposes. The personal data is retained for a necessary period of time to comply with applicable laws. DATA SCIENCE AI LTD is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-parties or third-party websites.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They enable the website to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions. We use cookies for the following purposes: essential cookies (these cookies are necessary for the Website to function and cannot be disabled in our systems. They are typically set when you perform actions such as logging in, filling out forms, or adjusting privacy settings); performance and analytics cookies (these cookies help us understand how visitors use the Website by collecting data such as page views, traffic sources, and user interactions. This information helps us improve the Site’s performance and user experience). We keep this information for a period of 1 year and 1 month. Dfferent browsers provide different methods to block and delete cookies used by websites. You can change the settings of your browser to block/delete the cookies. To find out more about how to manage and delete cookies, visit wikipedia.org , www.allaboutcookies.org.

Individual rights

  • (c) right to erasure: you have the right to request the deletion or removal of your personal information under certain circumstances, such as when it is no longer necessary or if you withdraw consent. DATA SCIENCE AI LTD will notify relevant third parties of the erasure, where possible;

  • (d) right to restrict processing: you can request to ‘block’ or suppress the processing of your personal information in specific situations, such as when you contest its accuracy or object to processing. DATA SCIENCE AI LTD will inform relevant third parties of any restrictions, where possible;

  • (e) right to data portability: under certain circumstances, you have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and to transfer it to another party;

  • (g) right to withdraw consent: if DATA SCIENCE AI LTD relies on your consent for processing your personal information, you have the right to withdraw it at any time;

  • (h) right to lodge a complaint: If you have concerns about DATA SCIEBCE AI LTD’s privacy practices, you can report DATA SCIENCE AI LTD to the Commission for Personal Data Protection (CPDP).
